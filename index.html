About Us

Seattle Hadiya community unincorporated association is a nonprofit association organized by the hadiya nation in Seattle in 2019 to assist its member ... in an emergency, especially in covering funeral and related expenses for the deceased family. Also, the organization give non-monetary service to its member. Besides, the association provides moral and physical support to the affected family and establishes close social bonds between members and family members.

Our Members

The association has 35 members, and the number expected to grow in the next two-three years. The members are responsible for raising funds for the association once a year, and the amount they supposed to contribute decided by the management group. In addition to fundraising, the members are responsible for other social life-related obligation.

What We Do

The Seattle Hadiya community association's mission is giving relief to the members in a time of financial hardship that happened because of death in his family.
When we say family, it means the family of both husband and wife sides.
Even if the association's target is giving financial hardship relief to its member, the association also provides some non-financial service.
Non-financial service includes treating the deceased family psychologically.
